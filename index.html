Best Writer

R.K. Narayan

R.K. Narayan is one of the most celebrated Indian authors in English. He is best known for his fictional town of Malgudi, which serves as the setting for many of his stories1. His works often explore the lives of ordinary people and are marked by their simplicity and humor.

Mulk Raj Anand

Mulk Raj Anand is considered a pioneer of Anglo-Indian fiction. His works are known for their social realism and focus on the lives of the lower castes in India1. "Untouchable" is one of his most famous works, highlighting the plight of a young sweeper.

Khushwant Singh

Khushwant Singh was a versatile writer, journalist, and politician. He wrote extensively on Indian history, culture, and politics1. His novel "Train to Pakistan" is a poignant account of the partition of India and Pakistan.
